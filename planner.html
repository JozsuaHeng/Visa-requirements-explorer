Trip Visa Planner

Playground / v2 trial — pick a passport, a location, and a destination.

Pick your passport, then click any country (or use the Destination list) to see requirements.

Playground iteration — visa rules from the open-source passport-index-dataset (community-maintained, not an official government source). The "Location" field doesn't change the result yet — our data only models passport nationality, not physical location. "Search official source" links open a web search, not a guaranteed government page. Always verify before you travel.
